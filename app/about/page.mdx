# About Bible Verse Guessing Game

Welcome to the **Bible Verse Guessing Game**! This interactive game tests your knowledge of Scripture by showing you images that represent different Bible verses.

## How to Play

1. Look at the image displayed on the screen
2. Try to guess which Bible verse it represents
3. Enter your guess in the format: `Book Chapter:Verse` (e.g., "John 3:16")
4. Get instant feedback and learn the verse if you're not sure
5. Move on to the next verse and improve your score!

## Features

- **Visual Learning**: Images help you remember verses better
- **Hints Available**: Stuck? Click for a hint!
- **Progress Tracking**: See your score and accuracy
- **Public Domain Text**: Uses KJV translation (public domain)

## Verse Categories

The game includes verses from various categories:

- Salvation & Faith
- Comfort & Peace
- Wisdom & Guidance
- Strength & Courage
- Creation & Nature
- Love & Relationships

## Tips for Success

> **Pro Tip**: Pay attention to the themes and symbols in each image. They often directly relate to key words or concepts in the verse!

Try to memorize not just the reference, but the actual text of the verse. This helps deepen your understanding of Scripture.

## Technical Details

This game is built with:

- **Next.js** (React framework)
- **TypeScript** for type safety
- **Tailwind CSS** + **DaisyUI** for styling
- **MDX** for content pages (like this one!)
